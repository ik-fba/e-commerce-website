Book Description


"Letters from a Stoic" by Seneca is a collection of letters written by the ancient Roman philosopher and statesman Seneca the Younger. These letters, addressed to his friend Lucilius, serve as a guide to living a virtuous and fulfilling life in accordance with Stoic philosophy.
